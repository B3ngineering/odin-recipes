Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

A simple and delicious recipe for a peanut butter sandwhich - perfect for a breakfast, snack, or even dinner!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry jam

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jam on the other slice of bread
  4. Put the butter/jam faces of each slice of bread against eachother to create a sandwhich
  5. Eat
