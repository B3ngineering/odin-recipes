Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs are a quick and easy way to prepare a filling, high-protein snack!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Put 6 eggs in a small pot
  2. Fill the pot with water such that it just covers all of the eggs
  3. Set the pot on the stovetop and turn the heat on high
  4. Once the water boils, remove the pot from heat and set a timer for 6 minutes
  5. Remove the pot from the stove and rinse eggs in cold water
  6. Peel and cut the eggs, then pour a touch of soy sauce on top
  7. Devour
